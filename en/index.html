Our Range

Seven precast product lines

Interlock Tiles

Interlocking paving units for walkways, driveways and outdoor flooring — available in a range of shapes, colors and sizes.

Curbstones

Precast edge units used to define and reinforce the borders of roads, pavements and landscaped areas.

Solid Bricks

Dense, solid-cast masonry bricks offering high compressive strength for structural and load-bearing walls.

Hollow Blocks

Lightweight, hollow-core masonry blocks used for fast, cost-efficient wall construction.

Hardstone

Durable precast stone units suited for heavy-duty flooring and high-traffic surfaces.

Terrazzo Tiles

Decorative precast tiles with a polished, marble-chip finish for interior and exterior flooring.

Steel-Crete

Reinforced precast cement units combining steel reinforcement with cement for added structural strength.
