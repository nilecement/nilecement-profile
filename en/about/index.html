About Us

Building Egypt's Infrastructure, One Precast Unit at a Time

Nile Company for Precast Cement Products manufactures a full range of precast cement materials — from paving and curbing to blocks, bricks and decorative flooring — produced to meet Egyptian and international standards. Our production is organized across three sites, allowing us to serve projects with consistent quality, controlled color, and reliable strength across every batch.

What guides our production

◆

Quality

Every unit is produced against defined standards for size, strength and finish.

◆

Consistency

Controlled manufacturing keeps color and structural performance stable batch to batch.

◆

Reliability

Clear specifications and dependable output for contractors and project owners.

◆

Coverage

Three production sites in Abu Rawash, Badr Al-Rubiki and Suez support wider delivery.
