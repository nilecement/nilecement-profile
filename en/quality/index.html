Quality You Can Verify

Our products are manufactured under controlled conditions and tested against defined standards — not just claimed, but verifiable in every batch.

Standards Compliance

Product Components & Specifications

  • Ordinary Portland Cement grade 52.5, or white cement.
  • Sifted sand free of impurities, per ES 1109.
  • Graduated sizes of Attaqa dolomite crush, per ES 1108.

Production Quality & Techniques

  • Manufacturing with automatic hydraulic machines.
